Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026. This explains, in plain terms, what SallusTech does and doesn't do with your data.

The short version

SallusTech tools are designed to run in your browser. When you use a tool such as the PDF or image toolkit, your files are processed on your own device and are not uploaded to or stored on our servers.

Files you work with

Documents, images, and other files you open in the tools are handled locally by your browser. They are not transmitted to SallusTech, and we keep no copies. Closing or refreshing the page clears them from memory.

Information we collect

SallusTech does not require an account and does not ask for personal details to use the tools. Standard, non-identifying technical data (such as your browser type and pages visited) may be collected through aggregate analytics and standard server logs to understand general usage and keep the site reliable.

Cookies and advertising

This site may display ads served by third-party vendors, including Google. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, and you can review more options at aboutads.info.

Third-party links

Some pages link to external sites that have their own privacy practices. SallusTech is not responsible for the content or policies of those sites.

Children's privacy

SallusTech is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the contact page.
