About SallusTech

A one-person workshop for small, useful tools that respect your time and your data.

SallusTech is built and maintained by Salsabeel, an IT and e-learning specialist based in Ajman, UAE, with more than a decade of experience running learning technology and digital systems in higher education. Outside of work, he builds web and mobile tools and shares technology tutorials in Malayalam on the YouTube channel TechTalk by Sallu.

Why these tools exist

Most everyday tasks — splitting a PDF, shrinking an image, drafting a clean email layout, or working out a GPA — are simple, but the usual websites for them make you upload private files, create accounts, or sit through ads before anything works. SallusTech takes a different approach.

  • Your files stay with you. Wherever possible, tools run entirely in your browser using your device, so documents and images are never uploaded.
  • No friction. Open a tool and start. There is no sign-up and nothing to install.
  • Honest about limits. When a feature genuinely needs a server, it is labelled clearly rather than pretending to work.

What's here

The workbench currently includes a PDF toolkit, an image toolkit, an email layout designer, a media player, and a grade converter. New tools are added as they're built, and existing ones are refined based on how people actually use them.
